About us

Committed to bringing healthcare closer to you

Health Assure is India’s leading Primary Healthcare aggregator with a comprehensive network of 3,500 centres, set up across 1,100 cities across India. Our objective is to offer people all over the country, easy access to some of the top health care facilities.

In our endeavour to connect people to resources and create a healthier tomorrow, we uphold four core values.
